Nature Exploring

From a young age, my parents encouraged my love for nature, taking me on countless outdoor adventures. Over the past 15 years, I have explored some incredible places, but my favorite was Agawa Canyon Park. We took a train deep into the forest, spotting bears and deer along the way. Once we arrived, we hiked up the mountain, and the view from the top was absolutely stunning, it was truly a memory that I will always cherish.

Nature Exploration Spots I Have Been To:

  • Tobermory
  • Algonquin Provincial Park
  • Agawa Canyon Park
  • Point Pelee National Park
  • Turkey Point Provincial Park
  • Ojibway Provincial Park
  • High Park
